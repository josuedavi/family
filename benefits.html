Benefits of Families

  • Less Likely to Exhibit Behavioral Problems

  • Moral Character Development
  • Support During Difficult Times

  • Older Siblings Develope a Sense of Responsibility
  • Siblings Keep Each Other Company
  • Learning To Share
  • "Less Expensive Than You Think"(Reference: Cheaper By The Dozen)

Medium Families

  • Siblings To Play With
  • Not To Big or Too Small

Small Families

  • Very Close Bonds
  • More Attention From Parents
  • Financially Cheaper

Siblings
