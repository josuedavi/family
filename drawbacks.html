Introduction:

Most people have some form of a family, but what if you didn't? This is a reality for some people. You can have: no siblings, no parents, missing a parent, or missing any relatives. The effects of not having a family can be detrimental. Another type of serperation is foster care. Not having a family or being seperated from your family should be considered a huge social problem. Family is the first social introduction for most of us in the world we all know.

No Family Present

Without family you are alone, left to live and develop from your personal experiences instead of from parental wisdom. Lack of support leads to vulnerabilities and may lead to problems in society.

Pros:

  • Parent Gets Monetary Compensation
  • Many Resources Available
  • Emotional Relationships Can Be Started
 Cons:
  • Eventually, Most Get Returned To Birth Parents
  • Financial HardShips Of Caring For A Child
